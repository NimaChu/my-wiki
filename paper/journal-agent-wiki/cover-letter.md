# SoftwareX Cover Letter Draft

Dear Editors of SoftwareX,

Please consider the manuscript "Agent Wiki: A local-first evidence-closed
Markdown knowledge base for LLM agents" as an Original Software Publication in
SoftwareX.

Agent Wiki is an open-source, domain-independent knowledge substrate for LLM
agents and their users. It keeps source evidence and synthesized Markdown wiki
pages in separate local layers, derives a computable graph from ordinary wiki
links, and applies an executable evidence-closure check before a captured source
is treated as processed. The software also provides source capture, search,
maintenance queues, link repair, image-evidence indexing, optional external
imports, and a read-only graph dashboard.

The manuscript focuses on the software's motivation, architecture,
functionalities, illustrative workflow, and potential for reuse. A local
technical-documentation case study contains 1,137 raw sources and 127 wiki pages
with no unresolved links, orphaned wiki pages, or processed-source closure
violations under the included lint checks. The manuscript explicitly limits
this result to mechanical graph health and does not claim a controlled answer-
quality comparison.
